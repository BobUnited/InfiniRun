Instructions (expect at least 30 seconds for game to load):
The object of this game is to surive as long as you can.
You lose when your energy hits 0. You lose energy as time passes and whenever you hit an obstacle.
You gain energy whenever you collect a coin.
Press space to jump, press and hold space for high jump, press r to shrink/unshrink, hold f to float, and press g to flip gravity (only after you pass the 150 block mark).
